Introduction

Aims of project
Through this project, we aim to educate teenagers on the different cultures of the chinese dialect groups with the introduction of a website that acts as a platform for recipes of food of different Chinese dialect groups and information of various chinese dialect groups. We focused on teenagers as a survey done by us showed that majority of adults in 2015 are aware of the the different dialect groups and how they differ from one another.

Reasons for project

            Why did we embark on this project? The reason is simple. We wanted to educate singaporeans on different Chinese dialect groups in singapore. People would not visit a website solely containing information about chinese dialect groups as they do not have interst in that subejct. As food is a big part of culture, we decided to use recipes as a platform to educate people on the different dialect groups.

Our Project

Our project is a web app providing information on cuisines of each chinese dialect groups. Here, we show a variety of recipes from the different dialect groups with a write up of the history of the food item. With a user-friendly web application, it is easily accessible for anyone and everyone.

Research

In Singapore, many teenagers have expressed their dissatisfaction on the same types of food despite Singapore being called the food haven. Homecooks have also run out of ideas and have been repeating the same types of food over the years. With teenagers also losing interest in their culture of origin, we decided that SG50 was a good time to get teenagers to learn about the history of their own dialect group and also of the coexisting dialect groups.

Foodculture

Our app includes step by step instructions on how to cook each food item. Each recipe is categorized for the ease of people’s access, with this app we will be able to educate singaporeans on the different chinese dialect groups. The app is minimal and neat, with all the different recipes tag with general flavours, helping people who are seeing the recipes for the first time decide what they want to try. Included are instructional guides, recipes and a write up on the history of each recipe.

Future

As the web app is made to be dynamic, we will be able to update the app with ease. The feeback section allows users to request recipes that they want, and when we get enough requests we will update the web app with the new recipes included. Features such as "Most Viewed" and the search function is curretly unavailable as the server our website is hosted on does not have PHP capabilities.

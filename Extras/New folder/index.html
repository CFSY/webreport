Introduction

Aims of project
Through this project, we aim to teach young adults who are living alone some basic life skills that they will need when living alone. We hope that through this, they will be able to live independently without any external help from others.

Reasons for project

            Why did we embark on this project? The reason is simple. We found out that more and more young Singaporeans are unable to do a lot of simple things that they need when they are all alone living by themselves. They are completely lost. This project was started to give them a companion that can guide them through.

Our Project

Our project is a one-stop solution for young adults in need for help. Here, we compile all the essentials needed for basic things such as cheque writing, cooking and washing. With an user-friendly application, it is easily accessible for anyone and everyone.

Research

In Singapore, young adults are becoming softer and softer, causing the new generation to be labeled the ‘Strawberry Generation’. If not for NS, these people would be even more dependent on other people. Thus, to tackle this problem, we have created an app. We sincerely hope that this will help and benefit those using it.

La Vie

Our app includes step by step tutorials of basic life skills. Categorized and customized for the ease of people’s access, we hope that this app can help young adults in surviving by themselves. The app is simplistic and neat, with all the different life skills put into different categories. Included are instructional guides, recipes for both full meals and speed meals, and much much more.
